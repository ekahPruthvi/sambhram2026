About Sambhram


Sambhram, RVITM’s annual cultural extravaganza, is a celebration of joy, creativity, and community. Inspired by the Kannada word Sambhrama, meaning festivity, the event brings together over 1100 enthusiastic attendees for a vibrant fusion of tradition and modernity. With breathtaking performances, thrilling competitions, and an electric atmosphere, Sambhram is more than just a fest—it’s an experience that unites talent, passion, and heritage.
